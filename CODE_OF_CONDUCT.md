# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in GBSE a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, caste, color, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, and healthy community.

## Our Standards

**Examples of positive behaviour:**
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Respecting differing viewpoints and experiences
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully
- Focusing on what is best for the project and community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**Examples of unacceptable behaviour:**
- Harassment, trolling, or sustained personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Benchmark manipulation or deliberate claim inflation
- Attempting to bypass ATTA governance rules via PR pressure
- Any conduct reasonably considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to the maintainers through GitHub issues. Security vulnerabilities should use GitHub Security Advisories.

All complaints will be reviewed promptly. Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject contributions that violate this Code of Conduct.
